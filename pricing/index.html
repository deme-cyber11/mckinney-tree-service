What Affects Your Price?

Every tree job is unique. Here are the main factors that determine your final cost:

  • Tree size & height — Taller and wider trees require more equipment and crew time.
  • Location & accessibility — Trees near power lines, structures, or fences require extra care and rigging.
  • Tree species & condition — Hardwoods, dead trees, and diseased trees may require specialized techniques.
  • Number of trees — Multi-tree jobs often qualify for volume discounts.
  • Debris & cleanup — Hauling distance and volume of material affect the total.
