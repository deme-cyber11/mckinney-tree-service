About Prosper

Prosper is a rapidly growing town straddling the border of Collin and Denton counties, located just north of McKinney and Frisco along the Preston Road and Dallas North Tollway corridors. Once a small agricultural community, Prosper has exploded in population over the past decade as families flock to the area for its highly rated Prosper ISD schools, upscale housing developments, and rural-meets-suburban lifestyle. The town's newer neighborhoods feature freshly planted trees that need proper early care including structural pruning, staking adjustments, and watering guidance, while the remaining heritage trees on former farmland properties often require assessment and maintenance. NTX Tree Experts understands the unique tree care dynamics of a fast-growing community like Prosper and is committed to helping residents build and maintain beautiful, healthy landscapes.
