About McKinney Tree Pros

8+ years of trusted tree care for McKinney and Collin County

Who We Are

McKinney Tree Pros is a locally operated tree service company serving McKinney, Texas and the greater Collin County area. With over 8 years of experience and more than 500 completed jobs, we've built our reputation on quality work, honest pricing, and reliable service.

We specialize in tree removal, tree trimming, stump grinding, emergency tree service, tree health assessments, and lot clearing. Whether you need a single branch trimmed or an entire lot cleared, our team has the skills and equipment to handle the job safely and efficiently.

Our Commitment

Every job we take on is backed by our commitment to safety, professionalism, and customer satisfaction. We are fully licensed and insured, and we stand behind every project with our satisfaction guarantee.

  • Licensed and insured for your protection
  • Free, no-obligation estimates on every project
  • Same-day and emergency service available
  • Clean, thorough job sites — we leave your property better than we found it
  • Fair, transparent pricing with no hidden fees

Serving Collin County

While we're based in McKinney, we proudly serve homeowners and businesses throughout Collin County, including Frisco, Allen, Plano, Prosper, Celina, Anna, Wylie, Fairview, and Murphy.
