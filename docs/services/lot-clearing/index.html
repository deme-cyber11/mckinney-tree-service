Lot Clearing in McKinney, TX

Complete land clearing for construction, development, and property improvement — from single residential lots to multi-acre parcels.

Call for a Free Estimate

Clear the Way for Your Next Project

McKinney is one of the fastest-growing cities in Texas, and new construction is everywhere — from custom homes in master-planned communities to commercial developments along US-75 and the 380 corridor. Before any of that building can begin, the land needs to be cleared. McKinney Tree Pros provides professional lot clearing services that take raw, overgrown parcels and transform them into clean, buildable sites. We handle tree removal, brush clearing, undergrowth mowing, stump grinding, and debris hauling as a single, turnkey package.

Our lot clearing work covers the full spectrum of property types. Residential homeowners hire us to clear backyard areas for pool installations, detached garages, and outdoor living spaces. Builders and developers bring us in for new construction site prep, from single lot home builds to multi-lot subdivision clearing. We also work with property owners who have acquired overgrown or neglected parcels and need them cleaned up for resale, fencing, or simply better usability. Whether the lot is a quarter-acre residential plot choked with cedar and privet or a five-acre tract of mixed hardwood and brush, we have the equipment and crew to handle it.

We approach every lot clearing job with a plan. Before work begins, we walk the property to identify trees worth preserving, flag any protected species that may require city permits, locate utility lines and easements, and map out the clearing boundaries. If you want to keep certain shade trees while removing everything else, we can work selectively around them. Our goal is to give you exactly the cleared footprint you need — nothing more, nothing less — while protecting the features that add value to your property.

After the trees and brush are removed, we grind all stumps below grade, chip and haul all debris, and leave the surface prepared for the next phase of your project. For construction sites, we can coordinate with your general contractor or grading company to ensure the handoff is seamless. Everything we remove is recycled or repurposed: trunk wood is milled or sold as firewood, brush is chipped into mulch, and green waste is composted rather than sent to landfill.

Our Lot Clearing Services Include

  • Full tree removal — All trees within the designated clearing zone felled, sectioned, and hauled away.
  • Brush and undergrowth clearing — Dense brush, saplings, vines, and scrub cleared to bare ground.
  • Stump grinding — All stumps ground below grade for a smooth, buildable surface.
  • Debris hauling — Complete removal of all wood, brush, and green waste from the property.
  • Selective clearing — Preserve specific trees while removing everything else around them.
  • Grading preparation — Surface left ready for your grading contractor or next construction phase.
