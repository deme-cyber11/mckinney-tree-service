About Plano

Plano is one of the largest cities in the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex and a major economic engine for Collin County. Home to corporate headquarters for Toyota Motor North America, Liberty Mutual, and numerous tech companies along the Legacy business corridor, Plano blends big-city opportunity with a strong sense of community. The city boasts excellent Plano ISD and Frisco ISD schools, vibrant shopping and dining at Legacy West and The Shops at Willow Bend, and an extensive parks system with over 4,200 acres of green space. Plano's mature urban forest, much of it planted during the city's rapid growth in the 1980s and 1990s, now requires experienced professional care to address aging, storm damage, and disease pressures common in North Texas.
