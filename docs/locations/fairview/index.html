About Fairview

Fairview is a quiet, primarily residential town situated between Allen and McKinney in the heart of Collin County. With a population that has intentionally remained modest, Fairview is defined by its generous one-acre-minimum lot sizes, rolling terrain, and abundant tree cover that gives the community a distinctly rural feel despite its proximity to major suburban centers. Many Fairview properties feature decades-old live oaks, post oaks, and cedar elms that require knowledgeable, careful maintenance to keep healthy and structurally sound. The town's commitment to preserving its open, tree-lined character makes professional tree care an essential service for residents who want to maintain both the safety and beauty of their properties. McKinney Tree Pros is honored to serve Fairview homeowners who share our appreciation for healthy, well-managed trees.
