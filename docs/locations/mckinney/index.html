About McKinney

McKinney is the county seat of Collin County and one of the fastest-growing cities in the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex. Known for its beautifully preserved historic downtown, McKinney was famously named the #1 Best Place to Live in America by Money Magazine. The city blends small-town Texas charm with modern amenities, offering residents a high quality of life alongside excellent schools, expansive parks like Erwin Park and Towne Lake Recreation Area, and a thriving local business community. With a mature urban canopy and thousands of new homes being built each year, professional tree care is essential to maintaining McKinney's natural beauty.
