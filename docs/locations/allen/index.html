About Allen

Allen is a family-friendly suburb in the heart of Collin County, just south of McKinney along US Highway 75. With a population that has grown steadily over the past two decades, Allen offers top-rated Allen ISD schools, the popular Allen Premium Outlets, and outstanding recreational facilities including the Celebration Park sports complex. The city's mature tree canopy, particularly in neighborhoods developed in the 1990s and early 2000s, requires ongoing professional care to address age-related issues like deadwood accumulation, storm vulnerability, and root competition. McKinney Tree Pros helps Allen homeowners protect their trees and maintain the lush, green character that makes Allen such an attractive place to live.
