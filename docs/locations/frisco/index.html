About Frisco

Frisco sits at the crossroads of Collin and Denton counties and has grown from a small railroad town into one of Texas's most sought-after communities. Home to the Dallas Cowboys headquarters at The Star, PGA of America, and a booming retail scene along the Dallas North Tollway corridor, Frisco combines world-class amenities with a family-oriented atmosphere. The city's rapid development means newly planted trees need careful early care while established trees in older sections require regular maintenance to stay healthy in the North Texas climate. McKinney Tree Pros helps Frisco residents protect their investment and keep their properties looking their best year-round.
