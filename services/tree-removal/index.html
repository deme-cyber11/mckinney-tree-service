Professional Tree Removal You Can Trust

When a tree becomes a hazard, outgrows its space, or is simply no longer viable, professional removal is the safest course of action. NTX Tree Experts provides full-service tree removal across McKinney, TX, handling everything from small ornamental trees to large, mature hardwoods that tower over homes and power lines. Our licensed and insured crew uses industry-standard rigging, crane-assisted techniques, and sectional dismantling to bring down trees safely — even in tight residential lots with limited access.

Dead, dying, and storm-damaged trees pose serious risks to your family and property. Decaying root systems can cause sudden, unpredictable falls, while leaning trunks and hanging limbs threaten rooflines, fences, and vehicles. We perform a thorough on-site assessment before every job to identify structural weaknesses, determine the safest felling direction, and plan a removal strategy that protects your landscaping and nearby structures.

Our tree removal service covers both residential yards and commercial properties, including apartment complexes, retail centers, and undeveloped parcels. Whether you need a single dead oak taken out or a row of overgrown cedars cleared from a fence line, we have the equipment and manpower to get it done efficiently. Every removal includes full debris hauling so your property is left clean and ready for whatever comes next.

What's Included in Every Tree Removal

  • On-site assessment — We evaluate the tree's condition, location, and surrounding hazards before work begins.
  • Safe cutting & rigging — Controlled sectional removal using ropes, rigging, and crane assistance when needed.
  • Complete debris removal — All branches, trunk sections, and wood chips are hauled away from your property.
  • Stump options — Choose stump grinding or low-cut removal as an add-on to finish the job completely.
  • Thorough site cleanup — We rake, blow, and leave your yard looking better than we found it.
